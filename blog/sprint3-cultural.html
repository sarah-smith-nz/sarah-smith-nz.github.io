Emotional Intelligence Exploration

What is Emotional Intelligence?

How we deal with our emotions. Identifying our own emotions, emotions of others, and how we relate and communicate with others.

How is it different to IQ?

IQ is the traditional measure of how smart a person was.It was generally accepted that if you had a higher IQ you would do well in life. It includes measuring:

  • Visual and spatial processing
  • Knowledge of the workload
  • Fluid reasoning
  • Working memory and short-term memory
  • Quantitative reasoning

EQ is a measure of different things that are increasingly being accepted as being just as important, if not more important, in the success of ourselves. EQ measures someone's ability in:

  • Identifying emotions
  • Evaluating how others feel
  • Controlling one's own emotions
  • Perceiving how others feel
  • Using emotions to facilitate social communication
  • Relating to others

Why is it important?

Emotional intelligence is important as it helps us get along with others and cope in situations where emotions are running high. Understanding motivations and emotions of others is helpful in relationship building. It also helps us live lives with intention.
